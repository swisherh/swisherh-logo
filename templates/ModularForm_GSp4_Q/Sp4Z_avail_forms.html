The following table shows, for each even weight between 4 and 58, the Galois orbits of Hecke eigenforms in the space \(M_*({\rm Sp}(4,\mathbb{Z}))\). The number (#) next to each orbit denotes the number of eigenforms in the orbit.

  • E denotes an orbit consisting of Siegel Eisenstein series,
  • Klingen denotes an orbit consisting of Klingen Eisenstein series,
  • Maass denotes an orbit consisting of Maass liftings,
  • Ups denotes an orbit consisting of cusp forms which are neither Eisenstein series nor Maass liftings.
You can click on an orbit to get Fourier coefficient, Hecke eigenvalues, and other information.
