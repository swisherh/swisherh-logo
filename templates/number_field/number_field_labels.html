Labelling Number Fields

Each number field has a unique label of the form d.r.D.i where

  • \(d\) is the degree
  • \(r\) is the real signature (so the full signature is \([r,(d-r)/2]\))
  • \(D\) is the discriminant
  • \(i\) is the index, counting from 1 (in case there is more than field with the same signature and discriminant: for example 4.0.1008.1 and 4.0.1008.2)

The database consists of fields from two sources:

  • The PARI database from the Bordeaux PARI group
  • Additional totally real fields of degrees from 6 to 10 computed by John Voight.
