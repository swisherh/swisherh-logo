Discriminant Ranges for Number Fields

The database consists of fields from two sources:

  • The PARI database from the Bordeaux PARI group
  • Additional totally real fields of degrees from 6 to 10 computed by John Voight.

  • PARI database discriminant ranges. It is possible that the discriminant range depends further on the signature, but this is not shown here.
